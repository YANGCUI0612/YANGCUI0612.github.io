I am a PhD student in the [Laboratory of Functional Analysis in Silico](https://fais.hgc.jp/), [Department of Computational Biology and Medical Sciences](https://www.cbms.k.u-tokyo.ac.jp/en/), [Graduate School of Frontier Sciences](https://www.k.u-tokyo.ac.jp/en/), [the University of Tokyo](https://www.u-tokyo.ac.jp/en/), under the supervision of [Professor Kenta Nakai](https://www.hgc.jp/~knakai/). My research interests include bioinformatics, oncology, immunology, and molecular biology.
I am experienced in the analysis of bulk RNA-seq data, single-cell RNA-seq data, and spatial transcriptomic data.
