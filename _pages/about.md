I am a third-year  PhD student in the [Laboratory of Functional Analysis in Silico](https://fais.hgc.jp/), Department of Computational Biology and Medical Sciences, Graduate School of Frontier Sciences, the University of Tokyo, under the supervision of [Professor Kenta Nakai](https://www.hgc.jp/~knakai/). 

## Research Interests

My research interests include bioinformatics, oncology, immunology, and molecular biology. I am experienced in the analysis of bulk RNA-seq data, single-cell RNA-seq data, and spatial transcriptomic data.
